About This View

The Industry & Compliance Analysis view helps you understand how different NAC solutions address your industry-specific security requirements and regulatory compliance needs.

Key Features

  • Industry Selector - Choose your industry to see relevant compliance frameworks and requirements
  • Compliance Framework Coverage - Visual comparison of compliance coverage between solutions
  • Regulatory Requirements - Detailed breakdown of key regulatory requirements for your industry
  • Industry-Specific Requirements - How different solutions address unique industry challenges
  • Compliance Readiness Scorecards - Framework-specific compliance capabilities and gaps

Using This View

This view is designed for security, compliance, and risk management stakeholders to evaluate how NAC solutions meet regulatory requirements. You can:

  • Switch between industries to see relevant compliance frameworks
  • View detailed scorecards for specific regulations
  • Print the complete analysis as a compliance readiness report
  • Use this information to support security and compliance audits
