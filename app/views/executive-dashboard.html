About the Executive Dashboard

This dashboard provides a strategic overview of your NAC investment analysis with a focus on business outcomes, ROI, and competitive advantages of Portnox Cloud.

Key Features

  • Executive KPIs - Quick view of critical metrics that matter to decision makers
  • TCO & ROI Analysis - Detailed cost projection and return on investment visualization
  • Security & Compliance Comparison - How different NAC solutions support your regulatory requirements

  • Business Impact Analysis - Quantified operational and security benefits

This dashboard is designed for executive stakeholders and decision makers to quickly understand the business case for Portnox Cloud. You can:
